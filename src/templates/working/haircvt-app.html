HAIRCVT App

The HAIRCVT App is used by hundreds of hair stylists in London to manage their portfolios on the HAIRCVT Website.

My Role

I built the app using the Sencha Touch framework, with PhoneGap plugins enabling access to native functionality such as the camera, file transfer, and share sheets.

The app is available for iOS & Android, and has been used to upload thousands of hairstyles to haircvt.com.

Key Skills

  • PhoneGap
  • Sencha Touch
  • Web SQL
