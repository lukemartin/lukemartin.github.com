HAIRCVT App V2

The HAIRCVT App is used by hundreds of hair stylists in London and Paris to manage their salons and portfolios on the HAIRCVT Website.

Information on the first version of the HAIRCVT App can be found here.

My Role

Version 2 of the HAIRCVT App is a complete rebuild of the previous version, using the Ionic framework and Angular in favour of Sencha Touch. Babel was used to make use of the newest ES6 features.

The app is available for iOS & Android, and has been used to upload thousands of hairstyles to haircvt.com.

Key Skills

  • ES6 (Babel)
  • Ionic
  • Angular
  • Cordova
